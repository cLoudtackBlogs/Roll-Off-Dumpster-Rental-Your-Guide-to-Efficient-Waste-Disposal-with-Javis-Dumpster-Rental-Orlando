What is a Roll Off Dumpster Rental?

A roll-off dumpster rental is a type of container typically used for large amounts of waste. These dumpsters are transported by a specialized truck, and they roll off the back of the truck once it reaches the location. Roll-off dumpsters are especially popular for projects that produce large quantities of waste, such as home renovations, roofing projects, construction cleanups, and more. At Javis Dumpster Rental Orlando, we offer a variety of roll-off dumpsters in different sizes to cater to the unique needs of each customer.

  1. Convenience: Roll-off dumpsters are delivered directly to your location, which means you don’t have to worry about hauling trash to a dump. The containers are placed exactly where you need them, making it easy to dispose of waste as you go along.
  2. Versatility: These dumpsters can handle various types of waste, from household junk to construction debris. Whether you are cleaning out your garage or demolishing part of your home, roll-off dumpsters can handle it all.
  3. Cost-Effective: While there are many ways to dispose of waste, renting a roll-off dumpster can be a cost-effective solution, especially for large projects. You get the convenience of an all-in-one service without the extra costs that come with multiple trips to the landfill.
  4. Environmentally Friendly Disposal: When you rent a roll-off dumpster, waste is handled properly, reducing the risk of pollution. At Javis Dumpster Rental Orlando, we ensure that all materials are disposed of in compliance with local waste management regulations, which helps minimize environmental impact.
  5. Time-Saving: The convenience of having a dumpster on-site means less time spent dealing with trash removal logistics. You can focus on your project while we take care of the waste management.

  • 10-yard dumpsters: This is the smallest size we offer and is ideal for small cleanup projects like garage cleanouts, small remodels, or yard cleanups. It can hold up to 10 cubic yards of waste, or roughly the equivalent of three pickup truck loads.
  • 15-yard dumpsters: A 20-yard dumpster is a great option for medium-sized projects, such as larger home renovations, roofing projects, or construction debris. It holds about 20 cubic yards of waste, or six pickup truck loads.
  • 20-yard dumpsters: For larger construction jobs or significant home renovations, a 30-yard dumpster provides ample space. It can handle up to 30 cubic yards of waste, or roughly nine pickup truck loads.

Choosing the right size ensures that your waste management needs are met efficiently and that you aren’t overpaying for unnecessary space.
